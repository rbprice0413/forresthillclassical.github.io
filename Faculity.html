Headmaster Renfrow

At Forrest Hill Classical, we believe leadership begins with character—and no one embodies that better than Headmaster Renfrow. A wise and contemplative Golden Retriever, Renfrow has served the school faithfully since its founding. With a keen nose for truth and a wagging tail of encouragement, he sets the tone for an environment grounded in dignity, order, and the pursuit of virtue.

When he's not presiding over morning assembly, Renfrow enjoys afternoon strolls around campus, sniffing out philosophical riddles, and mentoring young students in the art of obedience and perseverance.

Renfrow reminds us daily that good leaders serve, listen, and occasionally nap beneath a sunlit window.
