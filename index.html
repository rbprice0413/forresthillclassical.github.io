Welcome to Forrest Hill Classical

Nestled in the heart of Fort Mill, SC, Forrest Hill Classical is a private school dedicated to providing a time-tested Christian education rooted in the liberal arts.

Our mission is to cultivate virtue, wisdom, and wonder in every student through classical pedagogy and a Christ-centered worldview.

Why Classical?

We believe that true education trains both the intellect and the soul. By returning to the sources—Scripture, great books, and enduring ideas—we help students grow into thoughtful leaders and faithful stewards.
